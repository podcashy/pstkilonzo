Expert Roofing & Rainwater Systems

At Roofers & Decor Interiors, we provide high-performance roofing systems designed for longevity and style. Whether you are building a new home or renovating an old one, our team ensures a leak-free, beautiful finish.

We specialize in Stone-Coated Steel Tiles and Decra-style roofing. These offer the classic look of traditional tiles but with the superior strength of steel—providing excellent weather resistance and noise reduction during heavy Kenyan rains.

Don't let a small leak damage your gypsum ceilings. Our repair services include structural timber reinforcement, leak detection, and iron sheet replacement to extend the life of your roof.
